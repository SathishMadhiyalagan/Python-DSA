Here are five different test queries with unique content for your API:

### **1. Product Review Analysis**
```json
{
  "text": "The new smartphone has an amazing camera, a sleek design, and a long battery life. However, the price is quite high, making it less affordable for many users."
}
```
**Expected Output:**
- **Summary:** The smartphone has a great camera, sleek design, and long battery life but is expensive.
- **Keywords:** smartphone, camera, design, battery, price
- **Sentiment:** Neutral (since both positive and negative points are mentioned)
